Ability Tags

Slugger
Ability excels in melee combat, is touch-based, or can only be used at close distance.
Slinger
Ability excels in ranged combat or can be used at long range.
Spy
Ability excels in espionage.
Helper
Ability excels in non-combat utility, typically used for emergency response heroes.
Soldier
Ability creates or relies on the usage of weaponry or equipment.
Whisper
Ability involves communication with nonhuman entities.
Builder
Ability creates artificial constructs.
Crafter
User can create advanced technology. Often paired with Thinker.
Smasher
Ability can be very destructive.
Doctor
Ability can significantly heal.
Bleeder
Ability can significantly harm.
Swindler
Ability affects others' sensory perception.
Hider
Ability affects other items' physical appearance.
Ruler
Ability affects others' bodily functions.
Scarer
Ability affects others' mental performance and emotions.
Seer
Ability affects the user's sensory perception.
Worker
Ability affects the user's physical prowess.
Mover
Ability affects the user's speed or reflexes.
Shifter
Ability affects the user's physical appearance.

Trait Tags

Thinker
User has innately increased intelligence.
Breaker
User has innately increased strength or durability.
Jumper
User has innately increased speed or reflexes.
Watcher
User has innately increased sensory perception.
Augment
User possesses nonhumanoid traits or a nonhuman appearance as a result of their ignition.

Special Tags

Combined (/)
Used when a specific tag cannot accurately describe an ability without another tag.
Asterisk (*)
Used when specific parameters (such as equipment) must be met for the ability to be used in that manner.

Affiliation Tags

Hero
Superhuman is registered with a hero agency.
Vigilante
Superhuman is associated with criminal activity acting in favor of upholding the law.
Villain
Superhuman is associated with criminal activity, actively acts against the law, and/or has been designated a wanted criminal by the Bureau of Superhuman Regulations.
Detained
Superhuman is currently detained in a superhuman correctional facility.
Unaffiliated
Superhuman is not registered with an existing hero agency, but does not commit crimes and is otherwise unaffiliated directly with crime or crimefighting.
